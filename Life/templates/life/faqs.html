What training and licensing do nurses and lab technicians receive?

Life hires the best pool of nurses, pharmacists and technicians. At Life, they receive theory sessions of 40 hours and practical sessions of 40 hours in the time span of a month. Once the training is completed, they work under supervision for 15 days and are then assessed on the basis of their performance. If all the qualifying criteria are met, they are permitted to work independently.
